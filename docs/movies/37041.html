Henry DeTamble (Eric Bana) is a Chicago librarian with an odd problem: he has a genetic disorder that sends him hurtling through time when he is under distress. In spite of this challenge, Henry tries to build a stable life with the woman he loves, Clare (Rachel McAdams). Though they are married, Henry disappears and reappears randomly throughout Clare's life. With the challenges of a regular relationship coupled on top of this difficult challenge, the two try to find a way to have a happy life together.
